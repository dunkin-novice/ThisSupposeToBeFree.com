The Story

I was just trying to optimize images for SEO — simple stuff. But every site I found either limited me or threw a paywall in my face. Eventually, I realized most of it could be done for free, right in your browser.

So I built my own tool… and it actually worked. That’s when I thought, why not share it? Let’s annoy the people who once annoyed us — and maybe make a little pocket money along the way.

And who knows — if more of us start building out of frustration, maybe we can make the internet a little more fun again. Paying for good software is fair, but come on… image converters? That stuff should’ve been free ages ago.

My Road (Support the Journey)

This project started out of frustration, but the goal is bigger. The dream is to turn this into something that not only helps people but also builds a real, independent, community-funded business. Your support is a huge part of that journey.
