"I'm just a marketing guy who got tired of 'freemium' limits."

Day in, day out, I was trying to optimize images for SEO. Every tool I found hit me with a paywall or a ridiculous daily limit. Turns out, most of this stuff can be done for free right in your browser. This site is my protest against the subscription-everything world, built for everyone who's been there. No limits, no uploads, no BS.

Recommended Tools

Some things are too complex for us to build. Here are some great tools—some free, some paid—that I genuinely recommend.

Heads up: Some of these are affiliate links. If you click one and make a purchase, I might get a small kickback at no extra cost to you. I only recommend stuff I've used and believe in.

Favicon.io The gold standard for creating favicons. Simple, fast, and does the job perfectly. A huge inspiration for this site.
PageSpeed Insights The official tool from Google to check your site's performance. Essential for any SEO work.
Canva For anything design-related. Their free tier is incredibly generous and powerful enough for most projects.
Namecheap Where I buy my domains. They're affordable, straightforward, and don't try to upsell you constantly.
